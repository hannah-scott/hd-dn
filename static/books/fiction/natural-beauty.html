Natural Beauty

Ling Ling Huang

2023-10-03

Having abandoned her life of classical music following a tragic accident, our obliquely-name narrator begins working for a cutting-edge beauty company that sells experimental procedures to the obscenely wealthy. But what is happening in the tunnels underneath the stores — what is the purpose of their bizarre compound/farm out in the desert — and why has her body started changing so dramatically? I really enjoyed this book, but there are two fantastic novels in here and sometimes they get in each others' way.
