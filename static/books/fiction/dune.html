Dune

Frank Herbert

2022-03-21T17:41:04Z

The story of court betrayal and guerilla warfare, holy revolution and the terror of one's own fate. While I liked the main cast, I found the support characters somewhat thin and the ending quite rushed. Compelling and wide in its scope and imagination, a nice blend of fantasy, history and science-fiction genre writing.
