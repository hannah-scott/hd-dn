We Had To Remove This Post

Hanna Bervoets

2023-08-23

A woman in a shitty financial position takes a job at a social media company: all day, every day, she moderates extreme content on the platform. But as the videos she and her colleagues have to look at become more violent, their relationships and their humanity become more twisted and broken. A really compellingly written, compassionately bleak story about the trauma we inflict on ourselves and each other.
