The Left Hand of Darkness

Ursula K. Le Guin

A very well-written and moving book — I only wanted it to have been more of everything it was. Astonishingly beautiful in parts. I'm very deeply glad I read this. Particularly the final push across the ice, the descriptions and imagery were fantastic.
