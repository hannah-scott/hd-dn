Harrow the Ninth

Tamsyn Muir

2023-06-06

Fantastic follow-up, I think I maybe even preferred this to Gideon. The structure of this book — half second-person follow-on, half a bizarro retelling of Gideon — was immediately compelling, and Muir navigated it so well. I really want this series to stick the landing.
