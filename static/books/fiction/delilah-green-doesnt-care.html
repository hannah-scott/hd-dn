Delilah Green Doesn't Care

Ashley Herring Blake

2023-10-09

A queer photographer is dragged back to her hometown to shoot the wedding of her estranged step-sister. But both she and the bridesmaid are immediately physically attracted to each other, and as they try to keep their newfound love a secret they struggle with another problem: the shit-head groom-to-be. A compassionate, sexy, kind-hearted story about the secrets we keep from each other and the pain and beauty of letting people into our lives.
