Boy Parts

Eliza Clark

2023-11-26

Irina is a fetish photographer preparing for an upcoming exhibition on her subect of choice: boys. But as she works — scouting models off the streets, pushing artistic boundaries, luxuriating in her transgression — one boy's face keeps forcing itself back into her thoughts. A wonderful and sick novel, drawing threads between Sontag's violence of the image & the omnipresent violence of systemic misogyny, wealth inequality, homophobia, racism and addiction.
