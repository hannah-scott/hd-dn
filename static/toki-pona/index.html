Toki Pona

I really love the constructed language Toki Pona. I find it fun to play around with, communicate in, and even just do housework while talking to myself in it.

This is a selection of pages on my site that I've tried to translate into Toki Pona. Hopefully I'll keep expanding this, and maybe even have a full translation of the site into Toki Pona!

There's also tenpo ni, which is a little semi-regular diary I keep. Seems like a great way to work on my creative writing in toki pona.
