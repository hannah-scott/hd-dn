Film Photography

I used to shoot a lot of film between the ages of 16 and 18, very cheap C41 colour film. I got out of the habit at uni. I found my cameras while going through my old things, and have decided to learn how to develop.

At the moment I'm using a Nikon Nikomat FT. It belonged to my granddad. I've had to fix some leaks and the light meter doesn't work, but I love it.

I developed these pictures myself at home and scanned them in with a pretty crappy little Kodak negative scanner. All the dates refer to when I developed the roll.
