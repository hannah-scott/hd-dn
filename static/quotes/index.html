Quotes

I've collected quotes that speak to me for a while now. They can presage important things in my life — as I realised I was trans, I noticed many of the quotes I'd collected were about some sort of transition:

And the day came when the risk to remain tight in a bud was more painful than the risk it took to blossom.
– Lassie Benton

The beauty of writing is in reading, finding a phrase that feels true and valuable to you. Well-written words can serve as guidestones, leading you towards the life you want — sometimes it can be a life you didn't even know you wanted. These quotes are a map. I hope they take me somewhere wonderful.

I hope you find something that excites you here. If you have your own list of quotes, send it to me at hannah@hd-dn.com — I'd love to read it.
