Boy was that a depressing first post! Wow!

I got a 410 open the other day — that was pretty cool. Does that make me an intermediate lockpicker? Gauging my own skill at something — that’s pretty hard to do. I don’t know if I can do that so well. As well, with a niche hobby it’s hard to know how good anyone else is.

I really want to teach someone to pick locks. I’ve got a couple of friends who might be into it. Mostly I just want to see friends. Covid has not helped my social life. I miss chilling in cafes and not doing anything all day, to be honest. It’s one of the things I loved about living in Paris — fuck, I’m glad I’m not in Paris now. Hopefully things will open up again, not long now.
