Hannah

Mar 30, 2021

So I’m starting a blog I guess. Very exciting. Love to blog.

I guess why I’m doing this is the feeling like I need to own my own space a bit more. Like a lot of what I do every day and encounter and stuff, I don’t own. Is that… I don’t know if that’s even a problem. Is it problem that I don’t own where I live, what I work on? No-one does, like we’re used to it, so I dunno if it’s even an issue. Maybe I’m making too big a deal out of it or something, who can say.

I bought Disco Elysium today — here’s something fun, I don’t own that! I bought the digital version, so I guess I own the right to play it on a PS4. I dunno, I feel like we’ve given up on ownership in the least cool way possible. Against a dream of people owning everything in common, nothing is owned by the people.

I was going to add, “anymore”. But it’s not really anymore. Nothing was ever owned by us. It’s not like things have changed. This is just how things are really.
