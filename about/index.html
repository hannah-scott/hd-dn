About me

I’m a big fan of making things. I’m a bigger fan of breaking things, forgetting to fix them and moving on to something else. My current areas of focus are badminton and testing out mindful approaches to personal tech.

HD-DN

HD-DN is my personal website, and my main online presence. I was inspired by seeing James Clear’s book summaries and Hundred Rabbits’ beautiful website.

I recently moved from handwritten HTML + Sass to a Hugo build and automated a lot of the conversion from HTML to Markdown, so there may be some weirdness.
