HD-DN

HD-DN is my personal site. It's written in plain old HTML with some Sass CSS on top. I tweak it a lot so if you come back and something looks different it's because I got bored.

This site used to be made with Hugo, but I didn't put in the work to understand templates enough to make things look the way I wanted. I may come back to Hugo.

Besides, there's a certain dumb charm to writing plain HTML.
