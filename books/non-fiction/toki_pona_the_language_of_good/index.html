Toki Pona: The Language Of Good

Sonja Lang

non-fiction toki pona

Sonja Lang introduces a microlanguage structured around minimalism and mindful awareness of the present. As an educational text, the quality of exercises is really high and the ideas are expressed clearly. This book didn’t take a long time to work through, and I really enjoyed doing it.
