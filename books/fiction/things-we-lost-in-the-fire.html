Things We Lost in the Fire

Mariana Enriquez

2022-05-27

A collection short stories set in the author’s native Argentina. I read this after The Dangers of Smoking in Bed, and for me this is less focused and impactful than the second collection. Still some fantastic stories, but less consistently a “horror” book.
