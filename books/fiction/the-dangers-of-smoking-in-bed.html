The Dangers of Smoking in Bed

Mariana Enriquez

2022-05-13

A collection of horror short stories set in and around Buenos Aires. Covers a range of horror genres: some of the stories instill this air of creeping dread, while others go all-out with disgusting and horrific imagery. Reminds me in terms of tone of Junji Ito’s short collections — really excellent.
