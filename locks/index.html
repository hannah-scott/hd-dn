Locks

I am not a lawyer, this is not legal advice

In the UK where I live, lockpicks are treated a lot like knives. While you are completely within your rights to buy them, it is illegal to carry them outside your home without a reasonable excuse. If the police search you and you have lockpicks on you, that can be considered as evidence that you were going to commit a crime.

I don’t agree with this law. It puts power and discretion into the hands of police officers, a group with a well-documented history of abusing any power and discretion they get. But we do have to navigate this law and deal with cops.

If you’re a professional locksmith on your way to a job you will be fine. For non-professionals and hobbyists it’s more vague. Personally I don’t carry picks unless I absolutely cannot avoid it, e.g. moving house. That’s a decision I have made for myself. Your appetite for risk may vary and I’m not going to tell you what to do.
