good trackz hannah likez

good trackz hannah likez is a monthly zine I write of — you guessed it — good tracks I like. I started doing it as a way to encourage myself to listen to a broader range of music, and think a bit more critically about what I enjoy in music.

I also hope it serves as a nice little time capsule of whatever I was listening to!
